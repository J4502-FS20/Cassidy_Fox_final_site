COVID-19 Education

Since COVID-19 hit Columbia, Missouri in March of 2020 everything has changed, including education. Now, our K-12 students are forced to learn at home on thier own technical devices. For a semester, my team and I followed Columbia Public Schools, it's teachers and students to learn how they are being forced to adapt.

Installment One
Back to school means first time face-to-face for new teachers and students

In August of 2020 Columbia Public Schools decided to start K-12 students remotely. This meant teachers were preparing lessons and teaching their students behind a screen. Maggie Robbins, a kindergarten teacher, tells us her experiences being virtual and her excitement to go back in person.
